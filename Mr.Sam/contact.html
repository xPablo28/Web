Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and working with us

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters related to security incidents, we have a dedicated emergency response team available 24/7. Please call our emergency hotline at +254 722 102 854 for immediate assistance with critical security issues.

Do you offer remote consultations for organizations outside your office locations?

Yes, we offer remote consultations via secure video conferencing for organizations across Africa and beyond. Our team can provide virtual assessments, training, and consultancy services regardless of your location. For certain services that require on-site presence, we can arrange for our consultants to travel to your location.

How can I schedule a demonstration of your security products?

You can schedule a product demonstration by filling out the contact form on this page, specifying the products you're interested in, or by calling our sales team directly. We offer both in-person and virtual demonstrations, depending on your preference and location. Our product specialists will guide you through the features and benefits tailored to your organization's needs.

Do you provide technical support for your products after purchase?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for all our products. Depending on your service agreement, this can include 24/7 support, regular maintenance, updates, and troubleshooting. Our technical support team can be reached via email at support@cybersecurityafrica.com or by phone during business hours. We also offer premium support packages for organizations with critical security infrastructure.
