Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our scholarship program

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline for the 2025-2026 scholarship cycle is July 15, 2025. We recommend applying early as the selection process is competitive.

Is there an age limit for applicants?

No, we welcome applicants of all ages who meet our eligibility criteria and demonstrate passion for cybersecurity. Both young students and professionals looking to transition careers are encouraged to apply.

Do I need prior cybersecurity experience?

While some basic IT knowledge is required, we welcome beginners with strong aptitude and commitment to learning. The program is designed to accommodate individuals with varying levels of experience, from beginners to those with some prior exposure to cybersecurity.

How many scholarships are awarded each year?

We award approximately 50 scholarships annually, distributed across different African countries. The exact number may vary based on funding availability and the quality of applications received.

Can I apply if I'm currently employed?

Yes, working professionals looking to transition to or advance in cybersecurity careers are welcome to apply. We offer flexible training schedules to accommodate working professionals.
